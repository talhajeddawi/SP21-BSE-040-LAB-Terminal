For Authors:
1. http://localhost:3000/api/authors (GET ALL AUTHORS)
2. http://localhost:3000/api/authors/objectIDofAuthor (GET BY ID)
3. http://localhost:3000/api/authors/objectIDofAuthor (UPDATE BY ID)
4. http://localhost:3000/api/authors/objectIDofAuthor (DELETE BY ID)
5. http://localhost:3000/api/authors (POST AUTHOR)


For Books:
1. http://localhost:3000/api/books (GET ALL BOOKS)
2. http://localhost:3000/api/books/objectIDofBook (GET BY ID)
3. http://localhost:3000/api/books/objectIDofBook (UPDATE BY ID)
4. http://localhost:3000/api/books/objectIDofBook (DELETE BY ID)
5. http://localhost:3000/api/books (POST BOOK)
6. http://localhost:3000/api/books/objectIDofBook/availableCopies (UPDATE AVAILABLE COPIES)


For Borrowers:

2. http://localhost:3000/api/borrowers/objectIDofBorrower (GET BY ID)
3. http://localhost:3000/api/borrowers/objectIDofBorrower (UPDATE BY ID)
4. http://localhost:3000/api/borrowers/objectIDofBorrower (DELETE BY ID)
